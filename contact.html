Most visits take 30–90 minutes depending on the pest and property size.

How are pests getting in my home?

Through cracks, utility penetrations and door gaps. Seal and maintain to reduce entry.

When is it safe to return home?

Usually 2–4 hours depending on the products—your technician will advise.

Typically after 3–4 days unless your service plan specifies otherwise.
